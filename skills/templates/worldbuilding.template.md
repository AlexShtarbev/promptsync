## RULES OF EXPOSITION

When introducing the world to the audience — **never through dialogue** ("As you remember, our magic works like this..."). Only through action, objects in frame, consequences.

A strong device: show the system in operation → the hero reacts to it as normal → the audience learns this is normal here.
